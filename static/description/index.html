Repair Discount Module for Odoo 13

Adds a per-line discount field to Repair Orders (Parts and Operations tabs).
Discount is applied before tax and updates the line subtotal and order total dynamically.

  • Discount (%) field on Parts and Operations lines
  • Subtotal calculation includes discount before tax
  • Order total updates dynamically
